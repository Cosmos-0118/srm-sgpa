Privacy Policy

srmcgpa · Last updated: June 2026

Overview

srmcgpa (the “SRM SGPA Calculator” browser extension) is designed to help SRM students calculate SGPA and CGPA on the official student portal. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what the extension does with your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal academic data.

  • Grades, credits, and course names are read only from the page you already have open on sp.srmist.edu.in.
  • All GPA calculations happen locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers or any third party.
  • The extension does not have a backend, analytics service, or advertising network.

Local Storage

The extension stores one preference on your device using Chrome’s built-in storage API:

  • Extension on/off state — whether you have enabled or disabled the calculator.

This setting stays on your computer and is not synced unless your browser syncs extension storage.

Where the Extension Runs

The extension only activates on pages matching https://sp.srmist.edu.in/*, and only injects its interface on the Provisional Results page. It does not run on other websites.

Permissions

  • storage — saves your on/off toggle preference.
  • Host access (sp.srmist.edu.in) — allows the content script to read the results table and display the calculator on that page only.

The extension does not request access to browsing history, cookies, passwords, or unrelated sites.

Third Parties

This extension is not affiliated with SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST). We do not share data with SRMIST or any other organization. Your use of the SRM student portal remains governed by SRMIST’s own policies.

Children’s Privacy

The extension is intended for students using the SRM portal. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the extension’s behavior changes. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any revision.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about srmcgpa, open an issue in the project repository or contact the extension maintainer through the channels listed there.
